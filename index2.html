Salient Venture Q4 Sales Analysis Using Excel

This project focused on analyzing Q4 2025 sales data for Salient Ventures, a fast-growing e-commerce company, to evaluate the effectiveness of its strategic turnaround initiatives. Following a consistent revenue decline across the first three quarters of the year, the company implemented key changes in Q4, including inventory diversification, performance-based incentives, and aggressive regional marketing. Using Microsoft Excel, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of transactional data to uncover insights across sales performance, product profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. I developed an interactive dashboard featuring key performance indicators such as revenue, profit, and profit margin, along with visualizations to support data-driven decision-making. The analysis revealed that high-margin categories like Accessories and Gym products were the primary drivers of profitability, while regions such as Texas significantly outperformed others. Customer insights showed that younger demographics and a small group of high-value customers contributed the majority of revenue. Based on these findings, I provided strategic recommendations focused on optimizing product mix, targeting high-performing regions, improving customer retention, and prioritizing high-margin products. This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw data into actionable business insights and support strategic decision-making through data analytics.

Aurora Threads Analysis Using Power BI

Aurora Threads is a premium fashion boutique with multiple stores, selling designer clothing, footwear, and accessories. Data analysis helps improve decisions, customer understanding, and sales growth. Total revenue is 288.46K from 771 items sold across 31 customers and three stores. Accessories generate the highest revenue at 148K, while clothing and footwear perform at similar lower levels. Handbags and necklaces are top-selling products, while watches and jumpsuits perform poorly. Unisex products generate the most revenue, showing stronger demand than male and female categories. Online payments dominate transactions, showing strong digital preference. Daily sales fluctuate with clear peaks and drops, showing inconsistent performance. A small group of high-value customers contributes most revenue, highlighting the need for targeted retention and engagement. Focus areas include promoting high-performing products, improving weak categories, expanding unisex ranges, encouraging online payments, using customer segmentation for marketing, and running consistent promotions to stabilize sales and improve overall performance.

The Omnistep Footwear Global Sales Analysis

An executive-level business intelligence dashboard designed for OmniStep to monitor key sales and operational metrics across multiple countries. The dashboard provides insights into total revenue, units sold, average fulfillment time, order volume, discount impact, payment method distribution, and geographic revenue performance. Interactive visualizations, including trend analysis and geospatial mapping, enable stakeholders to track business growth, identify market opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making. Built to deliver a clear, real-time overview of global footwear sales performance.
